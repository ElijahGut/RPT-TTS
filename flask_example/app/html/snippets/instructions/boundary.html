Perception of Spoken Discourse Meaning

Part II: Boundary

You will be listening to excerpts of speech of recorded conversations. You will be identifying spaces between words where the speaker has paused, however briefly.

Each page will contain an audio file and the transcript for the audio file, where underneath the space between each word there is a checkbox. For each of these utterances, mark the space if you feel the speaker made a pause there.

In each utterance, you will be able to select as many as you feel apply. Listen to the audio closely, as it can only be played twice.
